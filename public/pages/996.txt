see all my reviews lili taylor, as i think i've said here before, is one of my favorite actresses, but i have to admit i was hesitant about seeing this film, especially when a friend told me he found it overrated. i have a hard time with people who type people in general terms, and this movie seemed, at first, to say &quot;all men are scum.&quot; and the opening 15-30 minutes seem to indicate this is just going to be a rant. however, you do gradually get to empathize with all the people involved, and you do get an idea of how things were at that time. taylor and jared harris were fine as valerie solanas and andy warhol, respectively, but the real surprise for me was stephen dorff as candy darling. i've never thought much of dorff, but he really shone through here, refusing to stereotype candy or inject too much pathos. 5 of 6 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
