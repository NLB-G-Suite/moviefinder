an unpleasant slice of life 27 march 2006 | by gwailo247 (los angeles, ca) &ndash; see all my reviews i have a very hard time watching this movie. this is not a movie to put in for a relaxing night at home, as its very painful to watch. this is reality. these are situations that are happening right now in homes across the world.this movie is extremely powerful in its portrayal of a working class british family, as the struggle with problems such as drug and domestic abuse. the acting is simply amazing. it practically transcends acting and you feel that you are watching real people struggle through these things. its a reality show in which the people don't know they're being watched. this film is a tough journey to take, as its very painful and emotional, but it is ultimately rewarding to sit through it and reach the conclusion.very highly recommended, but be warned, this movie pulls no punches and shows you life as it is. 23 of 26 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
