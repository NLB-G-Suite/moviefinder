such is the case in this film starring no one you or me will ever hear of until a major blockbuster film comes out in which they play a minor role and eventually make their way to the top. this story has a bit of a twist to the usual tale, the 'bully' teams up with the 'loser' and they together save mankind. the humor is kiddie and not very often funny. but, the plot is well constructed and fine by my standards. 3 of 5 people found this review helpful.&nbsp; was this review helpful to you?
