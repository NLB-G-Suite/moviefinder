frequently asked questions this faq is empty. add the first question. user reviews &nbsp; uncommonly well put together classic thriller 15 february 2013 | by alannasser (united states) &ndash; see all my reviews thrillers on imdb tend to be evaluated by standards appropriate either for orson welles or hitchcock, or for contemporary high-budget endeavors. this movie has been rated as a mediocre, implausible contribution to the genre. i find this hard to comprehend. the movie has almost everything lacking in the vast majority of thrillers turned out these days (going back decades), and is far more artfully assembled -editing, direction, acting, pacing, modulated but effective suspense, and narrative leanness, not a trace of fat- than almost everything that&#x27;s come out since the turn of the millennium. the movie moves at just the right pace, the interweaving of the characters&#x27; motives and behaviors is artfully executed and the musicality of the picture&#x27;s dynamics is professionally effected. - it&#x27;s more engaging than most popcorn entertainments and a welcome addition to a genre that in these times seems to be in an advance state of decline. if you like well toned classic thrillers with an updated &#x27;70s touch, you&#x27;ll like this. 2 of 2 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
