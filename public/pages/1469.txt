edit storyline moving from the big city (la) to a backwater town is always difficult, but especially for the one doing the moving. after her dad's death, beth easton and her mother, kate, move to the house left to the family by a deceased aunt. kate meets up with several old friends, but beth has none. slowly, however, she makes friends, despite the lack of a nearby mall or anything else to do. soon she meets up with two boys fighting ... except one isn't a boy, but a girl. the girl, jody, is shunned by her peers as a "bad kid." as the film progresses, we see jody as the apparent victim of a bad relationship between her own widowed mother and ray, a man who, like everybody else, grew up in the town. somehow, beth sees that jody isn't all that bad;
