edit storyline anna mae bullock always had a special voice. soon after arriving in st. louis to live with the mother who had walked out when she was small, the now teenage anna mae soon attracts the attention of pop group leader ike turner. she becomes the band's singer, his wife, and mother to his children - not all hers. in love with ike and determined not to leave in the way her mother had, she finds herself the target of increasing violence from her unstable husband who can't see who is making the band such a success. written by jeremy perkins {j-26} plot summary | add synopsis plot keywords: tina turner | singer | violence | love | abusive husband |&nbsp;see all (72)&nbsp;&raquo;
