decent sequel to a very pleasant family film 15 june 2010 | by thelittlesongbird (united kingdom) &ndash; see all my reviews i really enjoyed the first film, it was sweet and cute, and elevated to a greater level by that wonderful killer whale keiko who played willy. this sequel is not as good, but it is decent. i do agree the story isn&#x27;t as good or as believable this time around, the script lacks sparkle and the direction isn&#x27;t as clever. but it is beautifully filmed, with lovely cinematography and scenery, and the score is really pleasant. free willy 2 mayn&#x27;t be the most fast-paced movie ever made, but it moves briskly, and the message is admirable. what makes the movie charming though is the cast, jason james richter is still very likable and francis capra is funny and natural here. mary kate schellhart and michael madson also do nice work, however it is the orcas that steal the show, every scene with them in is utterly riveting. overall, inferior but nice and pleasant. 7/10 bethany cox 2 of 4 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
