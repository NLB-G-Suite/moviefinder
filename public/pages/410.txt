edit storyline benjy stone is the junior writer on the top rated variety/comedy show, in the mid 50s (the early years). its a new medium and the rules were not fully established. alan swann, an erol flynn type actor with a drinking problem is to be that weeks guest star. when king kaiser, the headliner wants to throw swann off the show, benjy makes a pitch to save his childhood hero, and is made swann's babysitter. on top of this, a union boss doesn't care for kaiser's parody of him and has plans to stop the show. written by john vogel &lt;jlvogel@comcast.net&gt;
