see all my reviews &#39;the beautician and the beast&#39; follows the line of the immensely &#39;the sound of music&#39; and drescher&#39;s own popular sitcom &#39;the nanny&#39;. it has the usual mushiness and fluffiness that other american rom-coms have (although this one is set in eastern europe) and it&#39;s accompanied by a nice soundtrack. however, what really sets this one apart is the delightfully funny fran drescher. her character is no different from her unique persona as nanny&#39;s fran fine but even so, her wit, charisma and comic timing are perfect. those who find her annoying in &#39;the nanny&#39; would most likely hate this movie but for me, this actress makes it somewhat likable. timothy dalton as the beautician (just kidding) is quite adequate (his fake accent is hilarious) and both actors share a good chemistry. the rest are just okay, mostly gap fillers. there is nothing path-breaking about this movie but if one is a fan of ms. drescher then why not give this a try if you&#39;ve got nothing else to lose? 11 of 12 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
