romantic, charming... and oh so french. 11 september 2004 | by quicksand (united states) &ndash; see all my reviews a sweet little romantic comedy; inherently conservative at its core, but overall it's just so charming and sweet you can't do anything but smile through most of it. i don't know much french, but the credits tell me &quot;a la mode&quot; translates to &quot;in fashion,&quot; and not &quot;with ice cream,&quot; as was my first guess.it's fluff, but it's french fluff, so if you're into romantic comedies and want something a little different, this might make a nice rental. very french, r-rated (one scene of nudity), wacky fun that melts as soon as it's over, but is well worth the experience. 8/10, though that goes up or down depending on your enjoyment of fabric. 7 of 7 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
