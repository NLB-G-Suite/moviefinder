see all my reviews this is yet another romantic comedy that wants to get by on charm rather than anything genuinely funny or romantic, but surprisingly, for the most part, it does. part of that is due to the fact that everyone in the cast is likable, particularly jennifer anniston, who i'm not a big fan of, and jay mohr, who i like but aside from jerry maguire haven't seen much of. also, director glenn gordon caron may be working with an outrageous concept, as most romantic comedies these days do, but he tries to keep it grounded, which is nice. i'd recommend it more except i hated the ending;
