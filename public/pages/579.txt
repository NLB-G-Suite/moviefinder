frequently asked questions this faq is empty. add the first question. user reviews funny, offbeat movie with a real message, 7 of 10. 17 july 2000 | by txmike (houston, tx, usa, earth) &ndash; see all my reviews before i saw &quot;son in law&quot; i only had a vague idea who pauly shore was. now i know. he is a superb comedic actor. in this movie he plays &quot;crawl&quot;, an upperclassman dorm counselor, who is a bit eccentric. he meets freshman &quot;becka&quot; from south dakota and they become fast friends. he helps her break out of her small town mold. in a thanksgiving visit to her parents' home, he pretends to be her fiancee to save her embarassment of a sort with an old boyfriend.i loved two things about this movie. pauly shore and the comedic situations are the first. the second is the message. how generations of &quot;men&quot; had perpetrated the same offenses against each other, and &quot;crawl&quot; helps them see a better way to treat each other. he gets becka's mom also to feel &quot;alive&quot; again, and rekindles romance between the parents. the whole movie is a fresh approach to comedy, is a joy to watch. i rate it a strong 7 of 10. 13 of 19 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
