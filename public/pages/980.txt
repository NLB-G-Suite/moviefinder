stagnant, overlong crime drama. 23 february 2004 | by gridoon &ndash; see all my reviews &quot;keys to tulsa&quot; is not completely uninteresting - that would be impossible with the cast it has (including a made-up-to-look-like-elvis james spader and a hot deborah unger). but the story never seems to build and the movie drags from one purposeless dialogue scene to another; it goes on so long that it begins to resemble a soap opera. certainly the only two tense scenes are not enough for the &quot;thriller&quot; this was misleadingly promoted as.
