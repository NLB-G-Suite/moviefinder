edit did you know? trivia the paper currency that vito hands to signor roberto is historically accurate. the bills used are series 1914 large size $10 federal reserve notes. large size notes measure 7-3/8 by 3-1/8 inches compared to the small note, printed from 1928 to the present, measuring 6-1/8 by 2-5/8 inches. see more &raquo;
