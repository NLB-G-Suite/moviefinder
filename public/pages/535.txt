edit did you know? trivia while there are scenes that show r30 class subway trains running on the irt lexington avenue line, this would be physically impossible in reality. due to nyc's subway having been constructed by competing companies, trains for the lettered lines would not fit on the numbered lines as they are too wide. irt trains are narrower and shorter than bmt/ind trains. see more &raquo;
