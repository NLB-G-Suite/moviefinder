user reviews &nbsp; one of my all time favorites-stimulating to watch the incredible props and great actors. 20 april 2005 | by margmclane (united states) &ndash; see all my reviews i can see this again and again. music score- wonderful! reminds me of thomas newman's style, (road to perdition) but not him. he's got the sound of happy water down! my favorite part is the ingenious mechanisms used on the big boat/ catamaran type, the workings of the atoll-so creative. there are many lines &amp; terms that stand out for me....&quot; not for sale, not for sale?!&quot; &quot;this wee orchard&quot; is a 1-lemon bush-tree in a pot. tina majorino, here a small girl, is a strong, memorable character. her bond with the main character, the mariner, gives one a good feeling, one of hope about what's possible for the &quot;walking wounded&quot; among us. dennis hooper uses his voice to the max, to bring so much interest and color into the picture. the society on the big tanker makes me continue to wonder, to stretch - what would really happen, what would people be like, after generations had gone by, and overcrowding was a problem. great actors, all. interesting plot, cool props. makes one think, laugh, wonder. re: all the junk i've heard about this movie, and it's lack of promotion - i think its the non-viewers that have lost out. 78 of 112 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
