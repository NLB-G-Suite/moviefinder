edit storyline "niagra, niagra" begins quietly in a drugstore in poughkeepsie, where marcie, the film's disarming heroine, likes to shoplift. she literally crashes into seth, a quiet outsider, also on a shoplifting spree. marcie invites seth to accompany her to canada to find a black hairstyling head. they set off in seth's beat-up station wagon, destined for a toy store in toronto. while on the road, marcie confides to seth that she has tourette's syndrome, necessitating a series of detours to liquor stories and pharmacies along the roads of upstate new york. written by anonymous plot summary | add synopsis plot keywords: doll | pharmacist | pharmacy | police | tourette's syndrome |&nbsp;see all (80)&nbsp;&raquo;
