edit storyline the story of brian of nazareth, born on the same day as jesus of nazareth, who takes a different path in life that leads to the same conclusion. brian joins a political resistance movement aiming to get the romans out of judea. brian scores a victory of sorts when he manages to paint political slogans on an entire wall in the city of jerusalem. the movement is not very effective but somehow brian becomes a prophet and gathers his own following. his fate is sealed however and he lives a very short life. written by garykmcd plot summary | plot synopsis plot keywords: satire | british comedy | monty python | reference to jesus christ | spoof |&nbsp;see all (92)&nbsp;&raquo;
