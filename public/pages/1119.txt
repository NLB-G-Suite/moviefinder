edit storyline get on the bus follows several black men on a cross country bus trip to the million man march. on the bus are an eclectic set of characters including a laid off aircraft worker, a former gang banger, a hollywood actor, a cop who is of mixed racial background, and a white bus driver, all make the trek discussing issues surrounding the march, manhood, religion, politics, and race. written by robert drake &lt;robert.drake@index.com&gt;
