see all my reviews this is a hight quality movie, in that it's not like austin powers where it's just kind of silly, weird humor (and don't get me wrong on that, i loved austin powers). if you're looking for a movie with a good story line (and by the way there are some serious parts along the way that spices it up) and good quality humor, believe me, this is the one. it's hilarious and you will be laughing from your belly. it made me crack up. there's only a select few movies that dana's been in, but this one was one of his best. he really showed off his how funny he really is in this one, and, like i said, what's even more worthwhile about this movie is that it actually has a plot and it's a good one. 5 of 7 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
