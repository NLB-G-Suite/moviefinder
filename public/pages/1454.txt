edit storyline sissy hankshaw is born with enormous thumbs that help her hitchhiking through the us from a young age. she becomes a model in advertising and her ny agent 'the countess' sends her to his ranch in ca to shoot a commercial, set against the background of mating whooping cranes. there, she befriends bonanza jellybean, one of the cowgirls at the beauty- ranch. the cowgirls take command of the ranch from the countess and 'drug' the cranes with 'peyote'. the police besiege the ranch. written by pieter van scherpenberg &lt;mediavis@netland.nl&gt;
