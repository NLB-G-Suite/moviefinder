not for vh-1 25 february 2003 | by lynn (dreamerlyn) (denver, colorado) &ndash; see all my reviews as someone who only knew of carmen miranda as a caricature in cartoons and a halloween costume worn by friends, i found this film very enlightening. whatever might be missing in the film about the deeper details of her life, it is still an eye-opening documentary that at least shines some light on a real person behind the character. i thought this was a very respectful documentary and perhaps purposefully restrained for that reason. maybe more could have been done, but i for one am glad that at least this much was there for me to see. i have a new respect for carmen miranda and find that i'm motivated now to seek more about her on my own. 11 of 12 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
