see all my reviews this documentary did not only touched my heart, but also changed my mind forever. there is an old saying. what you see is changed by what you know, and what you know is changed by what you see. sometimes a person needs someone to light a candle to enable him or her to see in the dark. i never understood horses at all. it took six of us more than an hour to catch one horse before we could saddle him. i thought by myself, there must be a better way. from there the desire to understand these magnificent animals better. i didn&#x27;t know where to look for, or where to start. after a long search i saw this movie: &#x22;monty roberts: a real horse whisperer.&#x22; monty lit the candle for me and many others. i thank god for that and the fact that we got on a path to understand and communicate better with horses. what these lovely animals had to go through in the past, just because we never really understood them or could decode their language, ask for an apology. thank you monty for having the courage to light the candle. it wasn&#x27;t easy to light the match. ... and... yes. we don&#x27;t need six people and an hour to catch a horse anymore. now the horse catches us. thank you monty. you already made the world a better place for people and for horses. 1 of 1 people found this review helpful.&nbsp;
