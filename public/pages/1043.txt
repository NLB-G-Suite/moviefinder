edit storyline michael mccann is a man who feels totally betrayed by the world, after his wife revealed to him that their child was not his. a few years after his break-up we find him living alone in a small town. but, everything in his miserable life changes when he adopts a little girl whose mother died outside his house. after several years, the real father returns to claim the girl from michael, thinking that she will be an advantage for his political career. written by chris makrozahopoulos &lt;makzax@hotmail.com&gt;
