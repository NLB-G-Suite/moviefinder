goofs when sil ditches the bmw, a police officer finds it later and looks inside. it appears to be out of petrol, but the fuel gauge goes from r to f instead of e to f. however, fuel gauges on bmws (like most european brands) are labeled &quot;r&quot; for &quot;refuel&quot; to warn when the fuel tank is depleted. gauges bearing &quot;e&quot; for &quot;empty&quot; are customary on american and japanese marques. see more &raquo; quotes [first lines] xavier fitch: i'm sorry. young sil: [mouthing] i'm sorry. see more &raquo;
